Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade

This is the third installment in the Indiana Jones franchise and a sequel to Raiders of the Lost Ark. It is also the first Indiana Jones film to be rated PG-13. In the film, set largely in 1938, Indiana searches for his father, a Holy Grail scholar, who has been captured by Nazis.

This is the second installment in the Indiana Jones franchise, a prequel to the 1981 film Raiders of the Lost Ark.
